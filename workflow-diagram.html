Workflow Diagram

The diagram outlines the decision-based workflow of the Autonomous Guided Military Drone Module (AGMDM), ensuring adaptive and efficient operations. Starting with the drone's activation, it evaluates key conditions at various decision points, such as signal stability, target detection, exploration success, and the safety button's status. If a stable signal and detected target are present, the Autonomous Attack Protocol is triggered for engagement. In cases where a target is not detected, the drone initiates the Target Searching Protocol to locate one. If exploration fails or safety conditions demand it, the De-Activation Protocol safely halts operations. The workflow also accommodates operator intervention, allowing for Manual Control when the safety button is deactivated. This structured flow ensures the drone can dynamically adjust to environmental and operational variables while prioritizing safety and mission success.
